# Loop Healthcare

Loop Healthcare is a comprehensive healthcare management platform designed to streamline and enhance the delivery of healthcare services for patients, doctors, and administrators. Our system is built with the goal of making healthcare more accessible, organized, and secure, while ensuring a user-friendly experience for everyone involved.

## üöÄ Features

- **User Authentication:** Secure login and registration for patients, doctors, and admins.
- **Role-Based Dashboards:** Custom dashboards with tailored access and features for each user type.
- **Appointment Booking & Scheduling:** Patients can book appointments, and doctors can manage their schedules efficiently.
- **Medical Records Management:** Secure storage, access, and updating of patient records, prescriptions, and reports.
- **Doctor Directory & Profile Management:** View doctor profiles with specialties, availability, and ratings.
- **Prescription & Medication Tracking:** Doctors can issue prescriptions; patients can view and track medications.
- **Notifications & Reminders:** Automated reminders for appointments, medication, and follow-ups.
- **Health Analytics:** Visualizations of patient health data and trends.
- **Telemedicine Support:** Video consultation option for remote appointments.
- **Admin Controls:** User management, system monitoring, and analytics for administrators.
- **Secure Data Handling:** End-to-end encryption and compliance with healthcare data security standards.
- **Feedback & Ratings:** Patients can provide feedback and rate healthcare services.
- **Billing & Invoicing:** Integrated billing system for appointments and treatments.
- **Multi-platform Support:** Accessible via web and mobile devices.
- **Emergency Contacts & Support:** Quick access to emergency contacts and resources.

Loop Healthcare is continuously evolving, and future updates may include expanded analytics, integration with wearable devices, multilingual support, and AI-driven recommendations.
